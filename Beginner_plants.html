The Monstera

Variegated Swiss Cheese Plant

Shop Now

The Monstera adansonii variegata is a low-maintenance plant, perfect for beginners. It adapts well to bright, indirect light and only needs watering when the top inch of soil dries out. With its unique, hole-filled leaves and beautiful variegation, it adds a stylish touch to any space. Easy to care for and hard to overwater, it’s a great choice for anyone new to plant care.
